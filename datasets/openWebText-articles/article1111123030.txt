We provide a personalised music publishing service to a diverse and unique roster of artists, score composers and hit songwriters including Anna Meredith, Danny Brown, Daniel Lopatin, Equiknoxx, Khruangbin, Yves Tumor, Death Grips, Caterina Barbieri, Kwes, Miguel Atwood-Ferguson, Mount Kimbie, Morgan Geist, Boards of Canada and many more.
With the expertise we have developed over the last 30 years, we deliver creative opportunities for our artists from landmark synch deals and film scores to songwriting and collaborations.
