Police are on the lookout for a dog that bit a woman near a City of Falls Church park Saturday afternoon. If the animal is not located, the victim may have to undergo rabies inoculations, police warn.
According to Falls Church Police, the woman was bitten on the hand by an unleashed dog on Saturday, Jan. 4, around 3 p.m. near the Van Buren Street playground of Crossman Park.
