In this episode, we are joined by Wrestling icon Mark Schultz. American Olympic Wrestling champion, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt, UFC veteran, motivational speaker and writer. Mark was recently played by Channing Tatum in the Oscar nominated film Foxcatcher, a film based on Mark’s book that chronicles the events that lead up to his brother Dave’s murder. Mark talks about his decorated career, his transition into Jiu-Jitsu and what inspired him to write Foxcatcher.
