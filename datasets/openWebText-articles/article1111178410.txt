ESL have drawn the match-ups for the second day of the ESL One Cologne Offline qualifier.
The first day of competition in Katowice, Poland is over, as eight teams picked up much-needed victories, while eight will have to fight back for a chance to advance.
In the Swiss system, winners of day one matches will be facing each other on day two in randomly drawn match-ups, while the same applies for losers.
ESL have just completed the draw, which sees the favourites G2 matched to HellRaisers, while the rest of the day will offer potentially exciting matches such as Cloud9 versus MOUZ, TYLOO up against Immortals and more.
